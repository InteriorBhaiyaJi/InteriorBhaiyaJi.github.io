About Me

An interior designer with a wealth of experience in project management, sales, and marketing. With 8 years in the industry, I have honed my skills at renowned firms such as HomeLane, Livspace, Agarwal and Agarwal, and Kiwo Modular. Currently, I operate as a freelancer under the name InteriorBhaiyaji, managing my own turnkey projects, delivering end-to-end design and execution solutions.

My expertise includes managing all stages of the design workflow, ensuring creative and functional solutions that meet client needs. I excel in strategic planning, market analysis, and customer engagement, making me a valuable asset to a forward-thinking organization focused on innovative and impactful design.

My Mission

To redefine interior design by creating innovative, personalized spaces that enhance well-being and functionality, while building lasting relationships and exceeding client expectations through customer-friendly service.

My Vision

To be a leading interior designer known for transforming spaces into harmonious environments that inspire and elevate the human experience, while embracing sustainability and innovative design practices.
